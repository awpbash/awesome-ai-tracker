**[MemPalace/mempalace](https://github.com/MemPalace/mempalace)** ⭐ 42.2k 🆕 `Python`

MemPalace is a free, open-source AI memory system that stores your complete conversation history locally and makes it searchable through a Palace-inspired structure (wings, halls, rooms) rather than letting AI decide what to forget. It achieved a record-breaking 96.6% score on LongMemEval benchmarks by keeping raw verbatim conversations instead of summarizing them, so you never lose context from months of work.
